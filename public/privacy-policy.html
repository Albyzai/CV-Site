Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in the "Never Have I Ever" mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App does not require user registration, does not track your location, and does not collect any personal data. The App functions entirely offline-capable and only downloads game content from our servers.

Data Storage

The App stores your preferences (such as theme settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (Google) to deliver game content. Firebase may collect anonymous usage statistics. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App contains content intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
